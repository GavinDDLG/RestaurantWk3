Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

One of the luxuries to experience in traveling is trying some of the local places to dine in at. Some places may seem familiar with different restaurant chains, however, the quality and atmosphere may be different so here are some of the places.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. McDonalds
  2. J's Restaurant
  3. The Shack
  4. Himawari
  5. Plumeria Steakhouse

Mcdonalds | Chalan Kiya, Saipan

McDonalds, the place of birth for the Big Mac, McDonalds offers a variety of burgers and sides for everyone to enjoy. My personal favorite is the Doublel Quarter Pounder, just plain though. Whether you're looking to sit down and relax while eating, or you need something quick and on the go, McDonalds has you covered. 

What I Love:
  • Friendly Atmosphere
  • Quick Food Service
  • Accommodating Options
